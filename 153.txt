It was an old Gibson guitar, sandpapered and waxed to a honey yellow finish. Another sort of instrument lay beside it—a twelve-gauge pump-action shotgun, brand-new, blue barreled, and with a sportsman’s scene of pheasants in flight etched along the stock.
